Core Philosophy
The Offline-First Mandate
RepForgee is engineered with a Privacy-by-Hardening philosophy. Unlike conventional fitness platforms, your kinetic data — reps, sets, weight, and movement patterns — is primary-stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your raw workout logs to any server unless you explicitly trigger an "Elite Cloud Sync."

No account required. No email. No phone number. No identity. Your data belongs entirely to you.
Zero Login Required
No account, no email, no credentials of any kind. Open the app and train.
Fully Offline
All features work without an internet connection. Your workout data never leaves your device automatically.
Local Encrypted Storage
All data stored via Hive local database on your device with platform-level encryption.

Health Sensors
Biometric Processing

Heart rate, SpO2, and VO2 Max data are processed inside the "Pulse Sandbox" — an isolated memory space within the app. This data is volatile and purged from memory every 24 hours unless you explicitly save it to your encrypted local vault.

We never access your device's Health app or HealthKit data without explicit permission, and we never write biometric data to any external service.
Location Services
Kinetic Location

GPS tracking for outdoor runs uses high-precision mapping to measure distance and pace. We never store a history of your "Home" or "Work" zones. All location data is obfuscated by ±50 meters before any optional social sharing is performed.

Location permission is optional. The app functions fully without it — outdoor run tracking is the only feature that requires location access.

Infrastructure
Security Architecture
RepForgee employs multiple layers of protection to safeguard your data even in the unlikely event your device is compromised.
Kinetic Crypt
All Hive database files are encrypted using AES-256 at rest. Your workout data is unreadable without the device unlock key.
Pulse Sandbox
Biometric sensor data is processed in an isolated memory space and never written to persistent storage unless explicitly saved.
Zero Analytics SDK
No Firebase, no Crashlytics, no third-party analytics. The app contains zero external tracking libraries.
No Permissions by Default
RepForgee declares zero permissions in AndroidManifest.xml. Location and sensor access are requested only when you use those features.
